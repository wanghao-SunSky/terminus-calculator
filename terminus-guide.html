Terminus Symbol Locations

The Research Office contains three crucial symbols needed for the Terminus puzzle. Here's where to find them:

Terminus Symbol Terminal

All three symbols (X, Y, Z) can be found on the main terminal screen in the Research Office. Look for the green monitor with the radar display, where you'll see three yellow sticky notes at the bottom showing the X, Y, and Z symbols.

How to Read the Symbols

The symbols are displayed on yellow sticky notes beneath the radar screen, labeled from left to right as X, Y, and Z. Each symbol shows a specific dot pattern that you'll need to input into our calculator.

  1. Enter the Research Office and locate all three symbols

  4. Click calculate to receive your unique code

Troubleshooting Common Issues

Wrong Code Generated?

Double-check that you've matched the symbols exactly as they appear in-game. Even small variations can lead to incorrect results.

Symbols Not Visible?

Ensure you've powered on the Research Office and activated all necessary terminals first.

Code Not Working?

Wait for the next round to begin before entering the code. The terminal resets between rounds.
